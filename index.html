About Me

I develop computational tools for mass spectrometry data processing and analysis. My expertise lies in metabolomics and lipidomics, integrating experimental LC/MS workflows with efficient data-driven approaches. Passionate about automation and efficient data handling, I work with Python, Polars, and parallel processing to make mass spectrometry data more accessible.
